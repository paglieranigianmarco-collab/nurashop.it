1. EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
Nura is Italy's first curated longevity and wellness marketplace, designed to solve a critical market gap for Italian consumers seeking premium international supplements and wellness products.
THE PROBLEM
Must navigate multiple international websites with language barriers
High shipping costs and variable delivery times from foreign suppliers
Customs complications and potential import delays
No centralized Italian-language customer support
Lack of trusted curation—hard to distinguish quality from hype
THE SOLUTION
Single-destination marketplace with pre-vetted, premium international brands
Subscription discounts (15-20% off) for recurring customers
Free shipping on orders over €50
Italian-language website, product descriptions, and customer support
Personalization via science-backed quiz
Content hub with Italian-language longevity education
MARKET OPPORTUNITY
Italian wellness market: €4.5B+ annually
Supplement segment growth: 8-10% annually
Benchmark: Healf.com (UK) valued at €100M+, 5,000+ SKUs
REVENUE MODEL
Product margins: 25-40% markup on wholesale
Subscription fees: Premium members pay for monthly/annual subscriptions
Affiliate commissions: Partnerships with health creators
Data & insights: Anonymized longevity trends for brands (future)
FINANCIAL TARGETS
Year 1: €50,000-100,000 revenue with break-even by Month 4-6
Year 2: €250,000+ revenue with profitability
Timeline: 1-2 months to MVP, soft launch Week 9-10

2. COMPANY FORMATION (SRL)
Structure: SRL Semplificata (minimum €1 capital, €400-600 notary fees)
Formation cost: €1,500-2,500 total, 2-4 weeks timeline
Process: Notary appointment → Camera di Commercio registration → bank account → Partita IVA → SCIA → INPS
Ongoing: Accountant €150-250/month for compliance

5. PRODUCT STRATEGY & SOURCING
Launch SKUs: 15-25 products across 5 categories (Sonno, Energia, Focus, Gut, Longevità)
Brands: Thorne, LMNT, Four Sigmatic, Vital Proteins, NOW Foods, Life Extension
Sourcing: EU distributors, direct brand partnerships, B2B marketplaces (Faire, Brandsdistribution)
Compliance: Register with Ministero della Salute via notifica (1-3 weeks per product)
Pricing: Wholesale +30-50% margin, subscription 15-20% discount
Model: Dropshipping to minimize inventory risk

6. LEGAL & REGULATORY
Regulations: D.Lgs. 169/2004 (Italian food supplement law)
Claims: NO medical claims ("cures," "treats," "prevents"); only wellness ("supports," "maintains")
Labels: Italian language mandatory (ingredients, dosage, allergen warnings, manufacturer info)
Registration: Submit to Ministero della Salute (€50-100 per product)
Data: GDPR compliance, Privacy Policy, Terms of Service (Iubenda €29/year)
Insurance: Product liability €500-1,000/year

7. FINANCIAL PLAN
Budget €15,000: Formation €2K, Digital €500, Inventory €4K, Branding €1K, Photos €500, Marketing €3K, Legal €1.5K, Contingency €2K
Revenue: Month 1-3 €2-5K/mo, Month 4-6 €5-10K/mo, Month 7-12 €10-20K/mo

9. OPERATIONS & LOGISTICS
Fulfillment: Dropship from suppliers (primary), alternatives ShipBob/Sendcloud
Shipping: Free >€50, €4.99 flat (3-5 days mainland, 5-7 islands via BRT/GLS/PosteItaliane)
Returns: 14-day right (EU law), 30-day guarantee (competitive advantage), expect 5-8% return rate
Service: Email + Tidio chat, <24h response, Gorgias integration
Team: Gianmarco (tech/marketing), Gianmaria (operations/suppliers)
Scaling: Hire part-time support €400-600/month at Month 6

10. TECHNOLOGY ROADMAP
Phase 1 (Launch): Shopify Basic + Dawn theme, Recharge, Klaviyo, Tidio, Judge.me
Phase 2 (Month 3-6): Shopify Standard, full Klaviyo, Gorgias, blog content
Phase 3 (Month 7-12): Quiz/personalization engine, AI recommendations, advanced analytics
Future: Mobile app (iOS/Android), community platform, B2B wholesale, health tracker APIs

11. RISK ANALYSIS
Regulatory: Engage Ministero della Salute early, maintain compliance documentation
Low traffic: Leverage 247 waitlist + aggressive Google Ads (target 3:1 ROAS)
Suppliers: Build relationships with 3-5 suppliers per product category
Cash flow: Dropship model eliminates inventory risk, manage marketing spend conservatively
Competition (Amazon): Differentiate via curation, subscriptions, Italian expertise
Product safety: Source reputable brands, verify certifications, maintain liability insurance
Burnout: Split responsibilities, hire support staff by Month 3-6

Week 1-2: SRL notary appointment, register nura.it, setup Google Workspace, create Shopify account
Week 3-4: Install Shopify apps, configure store (shipping, tax, categories), outreach to 5-8 suppliers
Week 5-6: Finalize branding, select 15-25 SKUs, negotiate wholesale pricing and terms
