KEY METRICS TO TRACK
Conversion Rate: (Orders / Visitors) × 100 | Target: 1-2% for MVP
Average Order Value (AOV): Total Revenue / Total Orders | Target: €50-75
Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC): Total Marketing Spend / New Customers | Target: <€20
Email Open Rate: (Opens / Emails Sent) × 100 | Target: 25-35%
Email Click-Through Rate: (Clicks / Emails Sent) × 100 | Target: 3-5%
Return on Ad Spend (ROAS): Revenue from Ads / Ad Spend | Target: 3:1 or higher
Customer Lifetime Value (LTV): Avg Revenue × Retention Period | Target: €150-300

SUCCESS FACTORS & KEY TIPS
Speed Over Perfection: Launch with 15-20 products, not 100. Iterate based on demand.
Focus on Waitlist: Convert your 247 waitlist members first with special launch offers.
Daily Operations: Spend 15 minutes each morning checking orders, emails, analytics.
Weekly Sync: Sunday 30-min meeting: review KPIs, blockers, next week priorities.
Document Everything: Every decision, meeting, supplier contact, bug fix - document it.
Parallel Execution: While waiting for notary, start supplier research. Maximize time.
Budget Discipline: €15K is runway. Track daily, optimize weekly, cut ruthlessly.
Early Customer Feedback: Call waitlist members in Week 2 - learn what they actually want.
