# 🚀 NURA — Company Launch Task List

> A comprehensive roadmap for launching **NURA**, a curated health & wellness e-commerce platform for the Italian market, inspired by [Healf](https://healf.com).

---

## Phase 1 — Foundation & Legal Setup

### 1.1 Business Registration & Legal Structure
- [ ] Choose the legal entity type (e.g. **S.r.l.** — Società a Responsabilità Limitata)
- [ ] Register the company at the **Camera di Commercio** (Chamber of Commerce)
- [ ] Obtain a **Partita IVA** (VAT number) from the Agenzia delle Entrate
- [ ] Register for **INPS** (social security) and **INAIL** (workplace insurance)
- [ ] Draft and notarize the **Atto Costitutivo** and **Statuto** (Articles of Incorporation & Bylaws)
- [ ] Open a dedicated **business bank account**
- [ ] Hire a **commercialista** (accountant) for tax compliance and bookkeeping

### 1.2 Intellectual Property
- [ ] Register the **NURA** trademark at the **UIBM** (Ufficio Italiano Brevetti e Marchi)
- [ ] Consider an **EU trademark** via EUIPO for future European expansion
- [ ] Secure the domain names: `nura.it`, `nura.eu`, `nura.com` (or alternatives)
- [ ] Secure social media handles across Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, LinkedIn, Facebook

### 1.3 Legal & Compliance
- [ ] Draft **Terms & Conditions** and **Privacy Policy** (GDPR-compliant)
- [ ] Comply with **D.Lgs. 206/2005** (Codice del Consumo — Italian Consumer Code) for e-commerce
- [ ] Set up a **cookie consent banner** compliant with Italian/EU regulations
- [ ] Understand **food supplement regulations** (Ministero della Salute notification for supplements)
- [ ] Verify labeling requirements for health products sold in Italy (Italian language mandatory)

## Phase 2 — Business Planning & Strategy

### 2.1 Business Plan
- [ ] Define the **mission & vision** (e.g. "Empowering Italian well-being through curated, science-backed products")
- [ ] Identify **core product pillars** (e.g. Gut Health, Sleep, Energy, Brain, Longevity, Fitness Recovery)
- [ ] Conduct a **market analysis** — Italian health & wellness market size, trends, competitors
- [ ] Define the **target audience** — demographics, psychographics, spending habits
- [ ] Create a **financial forecast** (revenue projections, expenses, break-even analysis)
- [ ] Define the **pricing strategy** and expected margins

### 2.2 Funding & Investment
- [ ] Calculate **startup capital needs** (6–12 months runway recommended)
- [ ] Explore funding options:
  - [ ] Self-funding / bootstrapping
  - [ ] Angel investors / venture capital (e.g. Italian VCs, EU health-tech funds)
  - [ ] Government grants & incentives (e.g. **Invitalia** programs, **Smart&Start Italia**)
  - [ ] EU funding programs (Horizon Europe, EIC Accelerator)
- [ ] Prepare an **investor pitch deck** with key metrics and growth strategy

### 2.3 Business Model Definition
- [ ] Define the **revenue model**:
  - [ ] Direct product sales (curated marketplace)
  - [ ] Subscription boxes / recurring orders
  - [ ] Membership program (personalized wellness, blood tests, AI recommendations)
  - [ ] Affiliate / influencer storefront partnerships
- [ ] Plan for eventual **omnichannel expansion** (e.g. partnerships with Glovo, Deliveroo)

---

## Phase 3 — Product Sourcing & Curation

### 3.1 Product Curation Process
- [ ] Develop a **product vetting framework** (ingredient transparency, clinical evidence, brand ethics)
- [ ] Define a **3-step curation process**:
  1. **Discovery** — research and identify high-quality brands
  2. **Expert Validation** — nutritionist/doctor review of formulations
  3. **Community Testing** — beta testers / early adopters feedback
- [ ] Identify initial **product categories**:
  - [ ] Supplements (vitamins, adaptogens, nootropics)
  - [ ] Functional foods (superfoods, protein bars, healthy snacks)
  - [ ] Personal care (natural skincare, body care)
  - [ ] Health tech (wearables, sleep devices, recovery tools)
  - [ ] Teas, infusions & functional beverages

### 3.2 Supplier & Brand Partnerships
- [ ] Research and shortlist **50–100 potential brands** (Italian and international)
- [ ] Negotiate **wholesale agreements** — pricing, MOQs, return policies
- [ ] Set up **dropshipping agreements** where possible to reduce inventory risk
- [ ] Establish a **supplier onboarding process** (contracts, quality standards, compliance docs)
- [ ] Build relationships with **Italian artisan brands** for exclusive products

### 3.3 Inventory & Logistics
- [ ] Choose a **fulfillment model**: in-house warehouse vs. 3PL (third-party logistics)
- [ ] Research Italian 3PL providers (e.g. **BRT**, **GLS**, **DHL Supply Chain**)
- [ ] Set up **inventory management** system (integrated with e-commerce platform)
- [ ] Define **shipping strategy**: standard, express, same-day (major cities)
- [ ] Negotiate **shipping rates** with carriers (Poste Italiane, BRT, DHL, UPS)
- [ ] Plan **packaging** — sustainable, on-brand, unboxing experience

## Phase 4 — Technology & Platform

### 4.1 E-commerce Platform
- [ ] Choose the e-commerce platform (**Shopify** recommended, as Healf uses it)
- [ ] Set up the **Shopify store** with Italian localization (€, IT tax rules)
- [ ] Configure **payment gateways**: Shopify Payments, PayPal, Klarna, Satispay, Apple Pay
- [ ] Install essential **plugins/apps**:
  - [ ] Recharge (subscriptions)
  - [ ] Klaviyo or Mailchimp (email marketing)
  - [ ] Yotpo or Judge.me (reviews)
  - [ ] Google Analytics 4 + Meta Pixel
  - [ ] Hotjar (user behavior analytics)

### 4.2 Website Design & UX
- [ ] Design the **brand identity**: logo, color palette, typography, iconography
- [ ] Build a **premium, modern UI/UX** (inspired by Healf's clean aesthetic)
- [ ] Key pages to develop:
  - [ ] Homepage (hero, categories, best sellers, brand story)
  - [ ] Category pages (Gut Health, Sleep, Energy, Brain, Longevity, Fitness Recovery)
  - [ ] Product detail pages (ingredients, benefits, reviews, certifications)
  - [ ] About Us / Brand Story
  - [ ] Blog / Wellness Journal
  - [ ] Contact & FAQ
- [ ] Ensure **mobile-first design** (70%+ traffic will be mobile in Italy)
- [ ] Implement **SEO best practices** from day one

### 4.3 Tech Infrastructure
- [ ] Set up **analytics & tracking** (GA4, Google Tag Manager, Meta Pixel)
- [ ] Implement **email automation** flows:
  - [ ] Welcome series
  - [ ] Abandoned cart recovery
  - [ ] Post-purchase follow-up
  - [ ] Re-engagement campaigns
- [ ] Set up **customer support tools** (e.g. Zendesk, Freshdesk, or Gorgias)
- [ ] Plan for future **AI-powered personalization** (product recommendations, wellness profiles)

### 5.1 Brand Identity
- [ ] Finalize **brand name, logo, and tagline**
- [ ] Create a **brand guidelines document** (colors, fonts, tone of voice, imagery style)
- [ ] Define **brand voice** — premium yet approachable, science-backed yet warm
- [ ] Commission **professional product photography** and lifestyle imagery

### 5.2 Pre-Launch Marketing

- [ ] Identify and reach out to **micro-influencers** in the Italian wellness space

### 5.3 Post-Launch Marketing Channels

- [ ] **SEO** — target Italian wellness keywords (e.g. "integratori naturali", "benessere intestinale")
- [ ] **Social Media** — Instagram, TikTok, YouTube (Reels, educational content, product demos)
- [ ] **Paid Advertising** — Meta Ads, Google Ads, TikTok Ads
- [ ] **Email Marketing** — newsletters, promotions, wellness tips
- [ ] **Influencer & Creator Partnerships** — co-branded storefronts (CreatorCommerce model)
- [ ] **Affiliate Program** — commission-based referral program for wellness creators
- [ ] **Partnerships** — gyms, yoga studios, wellness clinics, nutritionists

## Phase 7 — Launch & Growth

- [ ] Launch to a **closed beta group** (waitlist, friends & family)
- [ ] Gather **initial feedback** on UX, product selection, delivery experience
- [ ] Fix bugs and optimize the **purchase funnel**
- [ ] Collect **first customer reviews & testimonials**

### 7.2 Official Launch 🎉
- [ ] Execute the **launch marketing campaign** (social, PR, email blast, paid ads)
- [ ] Offer a **launch promotion** (e.g. 15% off first order, free shipping)
- [ ] Host the **launch event** (if planned)
- [ ] Monitor **site performance** and **customer support** closely in the first 48 hours
- [ ] Track and respond to all **social media mentions**

### 7.3 Post-Launch Growth
- [ ] Analyze launch metrics and **iterate on strategy**
- [ ] Expand the **product catalog** based on demand and customer feedback
- [ ] Launch a **subscription/membership program** (recurring revenue)
- [ ] Develop a **loyalty/rewards program**
- [ ] Explore **B2B opportunities** (corporate wellness programs, gym partnerships)
- [ ] Plan for **geographic expansion** (EU markets, starting with DACH or France)
- [ ] Explore **physical retail** presence (pop-ups, wellness concept stores)
